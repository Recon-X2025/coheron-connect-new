## Recommended Pricing Structure

### Pricing Philosophy
1. **Value-based pricing** aligned with comprehensive feature set
2. **Volume discounts** to incentivize larger deployments
3. **Feature differentiation** across tiers
4. **Competitive positioning** in mid-market segment

### Recommended Monthly Pricing

#### **Tier 1: Starter** (Up to 15 Users)
**Monthly Price: $299/month** (flat rate)
- **Per-user equivalent**: ~$20/user/month
- **Annual Price**: $2,990/year (save 17% = $598/year)
- **Target Market**: Small businesses, startups, small teams

**Included Features:**
- ✅ All 11 core modules (CRM, Sales, Inventory, Accounting, HR, Projects, Support, Manufacturing, Marketing, POS, Website)
- ✅ Up to 15 users
- ✅ 25GB storage
- ✅ Email support (business hours)
- ✅ Basic reporting & dashboards
- ✅ Mobile app access
- ✅ Standard integrations (5 integrations)
- ✅ Basic API access (1,000 calls/month)
- ✅ Data backup & recovery

**Why this price?**
- Competitive with Odoo Standard ($24.90/user) but with flat-rate simplicity
- Lower than Salesforce Professional ($75/user)
- Attractive entry point for small businesses
- Flat rate removes per-user calculation complexity

- 500 users: $1,999 + (100 × $12) + (200 × $10) + (100 × $8) = **$4,799/month**

**Why this model?**
- Flexible pricing for large deployments
- Volume discounts incentivize growth
- Competitive with enterprise solutions
- Custom pricing allows negotiation for large deals

## Additional Pricing Considerations

### 1. **Annual vs. Monthly Billing**
- **Annual billing**: 17% discount (2 months free)
- **Monthly billing**: Full price, more flexibility
- **Recommendation**: Promote annual billing for better cash flow

### 2. **Add-On Modules** (Future)
- Industry-specific modules
- Advanced analytics packages
- Custom integrations
- Additional storage ($10/100GB/month)

### 3. **Migration Services**
- **Starter/Growth**: Self-service migration tools
