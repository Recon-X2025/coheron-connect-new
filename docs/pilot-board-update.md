## Key Risks and Mitigations

| Risk | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|--------|-----------|
| Beta modules not ready for Stable by week 8 | Delays GA | Dedicated engineering sprints for CRM AI scoring, Marketing analytics, POS payments |
| Low pilot adoption | Insufficient validation data | Hands-on onboarding support; weekly check-ins with each customer |
| Performance issues under real load | Poor feedback scores | Load testing before pilot; monitoring and alerting in place |
