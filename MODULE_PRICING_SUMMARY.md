## Pricing Comparison: Modules vs. Plans

### Small Business (5 users, 3 modules)

**Option 1: Standalone Modules**
- **USD**: CRM ($109) + Sales ($89) + Accounting ($105) = **$303/month**
- **INR**: CRM (₹9,100) + Sales (₹7,475) + Accounting (₹8,775) = **₹25,350/month**

**Option 2: Starter Plan**
- **USD**: All 11 modules included = **$279/month** (save $24/month = 8%)
- **INR**: All 11 modules included = **₹23,300/month** (save ₹2,050/month = 8%)

**Recommendation**: ✅ Starter Plan is better value

## Key Insights

✅ **For 1-3 modules**: Standalone pricing may be cheaper  
✅ **For 4+ modules**: Plans are better value  
✅ **Add-ons**: 30-40% discount for plan customers  
✅ **Bundles**: 20% discount for module combinations  
✅ **Best Value**: Full plans when you need multiple modules

---

## Quick Decision Guide

**Need 1-2 modules?** → Use standalone pricing  
**Need 3-4 modules?** → Compare standalone vs. Starter Plan  
**Need 5+ modules?** → Use Growth/Scale Plan  
**Need specialized modules?** → Consider add-ons to existing plan  
**Starting small?** → Start with modules, upgrade to plan later
