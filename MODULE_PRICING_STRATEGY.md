# Coheron CRM/ERP - Individual Module Pricing Strategy

## Executive Summary

This document outlines pricing strategies for individual modules, enabling customers to purchase only the modules they need. This à la carte approach provides flexibility and can attract customers who don't need the full suite.

**Key Strategies:**
- **Standalone Module Pricing**: For customers who only need specific modules
- **Add-On Pricing**: For existing plan customers who want additional modules
- **Bundle Pricing**: Discounted packages for common module combinations
- **Exchange Rate**: 1 USD = ₹83.50 INR

## Standalone Module Pricing (USD)

### Pricing Model: Per-User + Base Fee

For customers purchasing modules individually (not part of a plan), we use a **hybrid model**:
- **Base Fee**: Flat monthly fee per module (covers infrastructure)
- **Per-User Fee**: Additional cost per user accessing the module

## Pricing Strategy by Customer Type

### 1. Small Business (1-10 users)
**Recommendation**: Individual modules or small bundles
- Start with 1-2 core modules (CRM + Sales)
- Add modules as needed
- Use add-on pricing when upgrading to a plan

### 2. Growing Business (10-50 users)
**Recommendation**: Growth plan + selective add-ons
- Base plan covers most needs
- Add specialized modules (Manufacturing, POS) as add-ons
- Better value than standalone modules

### 3. Established Business (50-100 users)
**Recommendation**: Scale plan (includes all modules)
- Most cost-effective option
- All modules included
- Advanced features unlocked

### 4. Enterprise (100+ users)
**Recommendation**: Enterprise plan (custom pricing)
- All modules included
- Custom development available
- Volume discounts

## Revenue Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Module-Only Customers (No Base Plan)

**Example**: Small business buys CRM + Sales + Accounting
- CRM: $49 + (3 × $12) = $85
- Sales: $39 + (3 × $10) = $69
- Accounting: $55 + (3 × $10) = $85
- **Total**: $239/month

**vs. Starter Plan**: $279/month (includes all 11 modules)
- **Savings with modules**: $40/month (14% cheaper)
- **Trade-off**: Limited to 3 modules vs. all 11

### Scenario 2: Plan + Add-On Modules

**Example**: Growth plan customer adds Manufacturing
- Growth Plan: $824/month (includes most modules)
- Manufacturing Add-On: $47 + (10 × $10) = $147
- **Total**: $971/month

**vs. Standalone**: Manufacturing alone = $79 + (10 × $15) = $229
- **Savings with add-on**: $82/month (36% cheaper)
